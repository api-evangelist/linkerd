Today we’re happy to announce Linkerd 2.19! This release introduces a significant state-of-the-art security improvement for Linkerd: a modernized TLS stack that uses post-quantum key exchange algorithms by default. Linkerd has now seen almost a decade of continuous improvement and evolution.
