<p>Welcome to the September 2025 Edge Release Roundup post, where we dive into the
most recent edge releases to help keep everyone up to date on the latest and
greatest! This post covers edge releases from August 2025.</p>
<h2 id="how-to-give-feedback">How to give feedback</h2>
<p>Edge releases are a snapshot of our current development work on <code>main</code>; by
definition, they always have the most recent features but they may have
incomplete features, features that end up getting rolled back later, or (like
all software) even bugs. That said, edge releases <em>are</em> intended for production
use, and go through a rigorous set of automated and manual tests before being
released. Once released, we also document whether the release is recommended for
broad use &ndash; and when needed, we go back and update the recommendations.</p>
